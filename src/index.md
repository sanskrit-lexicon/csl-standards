# CSL Standards

Technical standards and export workbench for CDSL dictionary data.

This site holds the TEI/OntoLex pilot that moved out of `csl-atlas`. Its job is
to test validation, publication, and modeling paths for dictionary records
without turning the public dictionary atlas into a standards workbench.

## Current Track

- [MW-PWG-PWK interoperability hard cases](/tools/interoperability-hard-cases)
- [`csl:` vocabulary index](/tools/csl-vocabulary)

## Goals

- TEI: validate CDSL markup and support publication for other lexicographic
  projects.
- OntoLex: stress-test dictionary modeling now; real RDF publication later.
- FrAC: stay frozen until VisualDCS or another corpus-evidence source is ready.
